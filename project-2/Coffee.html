◦ 02 / The story

Beans we know by first name.

We buy small. Most lots are under 200 pounds from farmers we visit once a year. Everything is roasted in a 5kg Probat in the back room on Tuesday mornings — you can usually smell it from the sidewalk.

If you want to see where your cup comes from, ask. We'll pull the green bag off the shelf.
